Recruiting Postcard

for the Davidson Honors College

As part of a university wide effort to increase enrollment and retention at the University of Montana, I worked with the Davidson Honors College to design new materials to distribute during recruiting visits. The goal was a clean modern design that used the university color themes and incorporated photos of actual DHC students.

  • Date: August 2016
  • Client: Davidson Honors College
  • Category: Graphic Design

Honors Experience Poster

for the Davidson Honors College

As part of a university wide effort to increase enrollment and retention at the University of Montana, I worked with the Davidson Honors College to design new materials to explain the path to graduating with honors. The goal was to create a simple graphical representation that made the requirements seem less daunting to students and improve enrollment and retention in the DHC.

  • Date: May 2016
  • Client: Davidson Honors College
  • Category: Graphic Design

Magazine Mockup

fitness magazine mockup for Intro to Illustrator Spring 2016

This project was for my Introduction to Illustrator class at the University of Montana. The parameters were to use Illsutraor to build a magazine from scratch with a cover, two-page article, and full-page ad. We could choose any theme or images that we wanted, but we had to create our own vector assets. We also made an ePub version of the magazine, complete with external links.

  • Date: April 2016
  • Client: None
  • Category: Graphic Design
